About Us

Why Studenter Room

We all know surroundings have great impact on our life and studies. Places where we live play vital role in this. Due to lack of information students struggle to find the perfect rooms as per their requirements. They do not know the locality and sometimes language barrier is also there, so they go to brokers and brokers charge certain amount either from students or from owner in both cases students have to suffer a lot financially. Luck play great role while searching for the rooms if they get good owner then all good else they have to stay there till bond gets over else the paid security amount will not be returned. it gets worse with middle class students as they can merely pay the fee.

Initial period of stays hostel/pg owner provide good quality of food to attract the students but when registration gets closed, food quality comes to its basic level. It hampers health of the students. instead of focusing on studies they start focusing on their food. There will be room for open feedback from student side. Accordingly action will be taken time to time.

Sometimes behaviour of owner also changes, they become soft to rude .some students are well pampered at home, this change in behaviour affect the mental state of students. It hampers the studies.

Main objective of our project is to overcome these problems of students and to provide a platform where they can select rooms and food by their choice without any hustle from their comfort zone with zero brokerage .
